      • Arrival at Baghdad International Airport pick-up , Go to the hotel to take a rest After rest and breakfast will start with liberation square monument, which commemorates the establishment of the Republic of Iraq and then we walk through Al Rashid Street is one of the main streets in downtown Baghdad. Its origin dates back to the Ottomans who ruled Iraq from 1534 to 1918. During that time, the only known public street in Baghdad was Al Naher Street , A great place for souvenirs and photography. Old market , Cooper market , Al Mutanabi street the famous book street with with the well-known tea house , Lunch in the most famous Baghdad kebab & Vegetable soup resturant , Visit the famous Al-Kadhimiya neighbourhood, with the night market and the Al- Kadhimiya holy Shrine (golden dome) , traditional dinner and back to hotel. After Breakfast will start early to Visit Al-Shaheed (Martyr) Monument. We going to visit the National Museum of Iraq , Reopened in 2015 after notorious looting during the war, it contains various treasure from the Mesopotamian and Babylonian periods. We will stop under Victory Arch to take a photo near to Arch , going to the archaeological ziggurat of Agargov, which is about an hour away from the center of Baghdad , We will spend there an hour and then return to the city center and get the famous Baghdadi food kebab , visit the greatest imam in Adhamiya and , going to the shrine of Sheikh Abdul Qadir Al-Gilani , The night is in the streets of Abu Nawas and Karada , and then back to hotel.
